About Alyssa

Hi, there! My name is Alyssa Kraus, and I am a junior at the University of Maryland College Park. I am a double major in multiplatform journalism and English.

Currently, I work as a broadcast production intern for Seacrest Studios and The Ryan Seacrest Foundation, where I host shows, create content, interview celebrities and guests, and design marketing strategies for programming.

I am also a copy editor for The Diamondback, the university's student newspaper, and a freelance reporter for several local newspapers.

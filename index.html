About Alyssa

Hi, there! My name is Alyssa Kraus, and I am a junior at the University of Maryland College Park. I am a double major in multiplatform journalism and English.

Currently, I work as a Seacrest Studios production intern for The Ryan Seacrest Foundation, where I create broadcast content for over 300 patients and their families at Children's National Hospital.

I am also a copy editor for The Diamondback and a freelance reporter for several local newspapers.
